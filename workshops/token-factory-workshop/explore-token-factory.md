# Explore Token Factory

## Runtime 

5- 10 mins

## Prerequisites

- [Nebius Token Factory](https://tokenfactory.nebius.com/) account

## Step 1 - Login to Token Factory

Login to your [Nebius Token Factory](https://tokenfactory.nebius.com/) account

## Step 2 - Explore the models

- Checkout prices and regions
- Filter models by type 'text to text' ...etc

<img src="images/models-1.png">

## Step 3 - Go to playground

Try out different models.

Start with simple prompts:

> write python code to convert csv to json


## Step 4 - Compare Models

On Playground, click on **`compare`** and try out 2 models side by side.

Give the same prompt and see how both models perform and how long do they take

## Step 5: Compare Reasoning

Compare 
- smaller models (e.g. `Qwen/Qwen3-32B-fast`)
- and larger models (e.g. `zai-org/GLM-5`)

Here are some sample prompts:

**prompt-1: Multi step arithmatic**

```text
Solve step by step.

A bookstore sells books for $12 each. During a sale, customers get 25% off if they buy at least 4 books.

Emma buys 6 books. After the discount, she also uses a $5 coupon.

How much does she pay in total?
```

**prompt-2: logic and deduction**

```text
Reason step by step.

Three people - Anna, Brian, and Chloe - each own a different vehicle: a car, a bike, or a scooter.

Clues:
- Anna does not own the bike.
- Brian does not own the car.
- Chloe does not own the scooter.

Who owns which vehicle?
```

**prompt-3(fun): test reasoning models**

```text
I need to wash my car.  The car wash is only 50 meters away.  Should I drive or walk?
```
